🕸️ Learn HTML in 5 Mins

HTML is the backbone of websites. Here’s a simple starter template:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
  <head>
    <title>My First Page</title>
  </head>
  <body>
    <h1>Hello world!</h1>
    <p>This is my first website.</p>
  </body>
</html>

Save this as index.html, double click it, boom 💥 website!

🧠 Lua Scripting Intro for Roblox

Here’s a script to print to the output in Roblox Studio:

print("Hello from Roblox!")

-- More: Connect this to a part
script.Parent.Touched:Connect(function()
    print("Part touched!")
end)

Put this in a part’s script and you’re on your way to scripting magic 🧙‍♂️

📱 Beginner App Dev Guide

Use MIT App Inventor (super beginner-friendly) or Flutter (more pro) to make apps.

Try MIT App Inventor:

  1. Go to appinventor.mit.edu
  2. Drag buttons, inputs, etc. into the phone screen
  3. Click “Blocks” tab to add logic
